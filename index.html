Our Mission

Founded in response to the lack of quantiative-focused student organizations at USC, Quant SC serves as an introduction to the dynamic field of algorithmic trading.

We foster an environment of knowledge and expertise through our algorithmic trading strategy incubator, where project teams collaborate on developing a trading strategy. And through guest talks, social activities, recruitment preparation, and more, Quant SC works to develop a true community of passionate and aspiring quants at USC.

What We Do

Guided algorithm-development

The core of Quant SC is our algorithm incubator. New members join every semester to learn how to create, test, and deploy their very own trading algorithm while collaborating with fellow USC students.

Recruitment preparation

Recruitment preparation events function as a starting point for anyone uncertain about how to enter the field. By demystifying the recruitment process, Quant SC hopes to make it a more inclusive, equitable process that develops the best talent.

Guest Speakers

Quant SC hosts industry speakers from professional firms to give members a glimpse of what professional life as a quant might look like.

Community Activities

Come to our game nights for community building through poker, blackjack, Fermi questions, and more!

Guided Algorithm Development Timeline

Strategy Research

Our members are taught all pertinent fundamental financial information necessary to analyze a potentially successful strategy. Teams research algorithms and discuss papers.

Implementation

With a given strategy picked out, teams will be given necessary guidance in how to select a development platform, read documentation, and develop implementations of custom trading algorithms.

Workshops

Workshops will be conducted for GitHub, QuantConnect, and ML to give students a practical introduction to programming.

Enhancement

All algorithms will incorporate detailed components that allow students to conduct original research and then determine the efficacy of algorithm component.

Evaluation

On an intra-organization team scope, project teams will be instructed in how to compare their algorithm's performance against others' and how to determine the best market niche their algorithm can embody. By learning to evaluate how their algorithm ties into the market, students will be able to further enhance their projects to meet their specific niche's characteristics.

FAQs

Does Quant SC accept members on a rolling basis?

Due to the structure of our project teams, we are unable to accept members outside of the application cycle.

What kind of time commitment should I expect as a project team member?

On top of attending meetings and speaker events, we want as much commitment as you can handle to produce the coolest, best quality algos possible! It sounds cliche, but the more you put in, the more you get out.

Can we continue developing our project after the semester ends?

Absolutely! Quant SC is all about you getting you to create and iterate. We encourage you to constantly improve your projects, as well as your relationships with fellow quants no matter the time of year.

In fact, once part of Quant SC community, members will always be welcome to attend future meetings or events.

I have an existing algo/idea I’ve worked on in the past, can I continue to build it in with my project team?

Of course! We're happy to help you continue developing whatever you're passionate about. Our resources are at your disposal.

My friend and I both got accepted! Can we pleeease be on the same project team?

We really are sorry but because of the specific needs of each project team, we cannot make any promises. However, we will consider any requests to be matched with friends or past collaborators.
